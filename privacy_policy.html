Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 30, 2025

LJK Gas Safe is committed to protecting your privacy and personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information in compliance with UK GDPR 2025 requirements.

2. Personal Data We Collect

When you contact us for plumbing and heating services, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Contact Information: Name, phone number, email address, property address
  • Service Information: Type of service required, property details, preferred appointment times
  • Communication Records: Records of phone calls, emails, and text messages
  • Payment Information: Payment details for completed services (processed securely)
  • Emergency Contact Details: Additional contact information for emergency services

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data based on the following legal grounds under UK GDPR:

  • Legitimate Interests: To provide plumbing and heating services, respond to inquiries, and maintain business records
  • Consent: For marketing communications (with your explicit consent)
  • Contract Performance: To fulfill our contractual obligations for services provided
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with Gas Safe regulations and safety requirements

4. How We Use Your Personal Data

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • Providing professional plumbing and heating services
  • Responding to service requests and inquiries
  • Scheduling appointments and emergency call-outs
  • Sending service quotes and estimates
  • Processing payments for completed work
  • Maintaining service records for warranty and follow-up
  • Ensuring compliance with Gas Safe regulations
  • Improving our services based on customer feedback

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for the following periods:

  • Service Records: 7 years (for warranty and legal purposes)
  • Gas Safety Certificates: 2 years (legal requirement)
  • Contact Information: Until you request removal or 3 years of inactivity
  • Payment Records: 6 years (legal and tax requirements)
  • Emergency Contact Details: 2 years or until updated

6. Sharing Your Information

We may share your personal data with the following third parties when necessary:

  • Gas Safe Register: For certification and compliance purposes
  • Parts Suppliers: When ordering specific components for your service
  • Emergency Services: In case of gas leaks or safety emergencies
  • Payment Processors: For secure payment processing
  • Insurance Providers: When required for warranty or insurance claims

We never sell your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

7. Your Rights Under UK GDPR

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal obligations)
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Request limitation of how we use your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Request transfer of your data to another service provider
  • Right to Object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for marketing communications

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at luis@ljkgassafe.com or + 44 (0) 7890 522123.

8. Cookies and Website Tracking

Our website uses essential cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Ensure website functionality and security
  • Analyze website performance (anonymized data only)

We do not use cookies for advertising or tracking across other websites. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

9. Data Security Measures

We implement robust security measures to protect your personal data:

  • Encrypted data transmission and storage
  • Secure payment processing systems
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Access controls and staff training
  • Regular data backups and recovery procedures
  • Compliance with industry security standards

  • Notify existing customers via email or phone

  • Ensure continued compliance with UK GDPR requirements
